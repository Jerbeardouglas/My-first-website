Architectural Perspectives Guide

Welcome to my guide about perspectives! Here we will learn about how to make your sketches look more natural and better. First, what is perspectives? Perspectives is an art of drawing solid objects on a two dimensional surface, it emphasizes the the right impression of their height, width, depth, and position in relation to each other when viewed at a particular point.

Perspective is quite hard to master, I, a learning student aswell find drawing perspectives difficult. But with time, patience, and consistent practice, we can master the art of perspective!
